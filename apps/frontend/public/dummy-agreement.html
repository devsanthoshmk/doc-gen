This Master Service Agreement is entered into as of October 24, 2024, by and between MergeX Inc. ("Company") and Client Alpha LLC ("Client").

1. Scope of Services

The Company agrees to provide executive advisory, consulting, and digital transformation strategy development services as detailed in the attached statements of work. Services shall be executed with reasonable care and standard professional skill.

2. Term and Retainer

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain in force for a period of twelve (12) months. Client agrees to pay Company a fixed monthly retainer of $8,500.00 USD, payable within thirty (30) days of invoice receipt.

3. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to maintain the strict confidentiality of all proprietary or trade information disclosed by the other party during the term of this engagement. Neither party shall disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written authorization.
